Memorandum of Understanding
ALUMNI PROMOTION - Florida

Welcome back to University of Phoenix. We are excited to have you as a returning student and wish you the very best as you continue the process of accomplishing your educational goals.

University of Phoenix would like to offer to its alumni entering into a master’s program who meets the following conditions:

  1. Is a University of Phoenix bachelor degree or master degree graduate;
  2. Successfully completes the first course in their second degree program with a passing grade as defined in the UOP student handbook.

The following benefit:

  • Waiver of the ™ (electronic materials) fee for all degree program courses attended for two years from the start date of the first course. This waiver will expire two years from that start date, without exception. Any courses taken after the two-year expiration date will not be eligible for the fee waiver.
By signing this document, you agree that: If you start a degree program under this waiver and are unable to continue your coursework at any time during the two-year period, the waiver will resume for courses you take within the two-year period. Any courses taken after the two-year date is reached will not be eligible for the waiver, and you will be responsible for paying the ™ (electronic materials) fees for those courses, within the standard University of Phoenix financial policies.

Please note that this offer only applies to University of Phoenix alumni enrolling from a bachelor’s to master’s degree or into a second degree program at the same level, so long as the second degree is within a different discipline of study. This offer does not apply to doctoral or certificate programs or to alumni progressing from an associate’s degree program to a bachelor’s degree program, regardless of where the associate’s degree was earned. This offer is valid for one degree program only and cannot be combined with any other promotion or offer. Additionally, employees of Apollo Group, Inc., its subsidiaries, their spouses and children are ineligible for this promotional offer.

Enrollment representatives are not authorized to make modifications to this document, either orally or in writing, and any such attempted modification is not valid.

Memorandum of Understanding

Associates to Bachelors Bridge Grant
Offer Available: April 26, 2013 – August 31, 2013

Thank you for enrolling again at the University of Phoenix (“University”). We are excited to have you remain a vital part of our University family and wish you the best as you continue the process of accomplishing your educational goals.

Eligibility for the Associates to Bachelors Bridge Grant (“Grant”):

The student must complete the following steps within the Offer Window of 12:00 am MST April 26, 2013 and 11:59 pm MST August 31, 2013 to be eligible for this program: 

  1. Be a student at the University of Phoenix currently enrolled in an Associates degree program.
  2. Sign this Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU”);
  3. Post attendance in the first course in their Bachelor’s program within the Offer Window and no later than 14 days from the official end date of the final class of their Associate’s degree program. 

The student must also earn a passing grade (A-D) in their first 3-credit Bachelor’s degree course.

Grant Benefits:

  • For all eligible students, the University will waive the tuition and electronic materials fees for the student’s first 3-credit Bachelor’s course.
By signing this Memorandum of Understanding, you acknowledge and agree to the following:
  1. That you must meet all eligibility requirements to receive the Grant.
  2. That if you start the first course in your Bachelor’s program more than 14 days after the official end date of the final class of your Associate’s program, or if you fail or withdraw from the first 3-credit course in your Bachelor’s program, you will become ineligible for the Grant and you will be billed within standard University financial policies.
  3. This is a non-cash award. No check or other cash monies will be disbursed at any time as a result and in no situations will the benefit exceed the invoice amount.
  4. This Grant may impact your financial aid eligibility and/or award.  If you have questions, you should contact your Financial Aid Representative for individual details as to how this benefit may impact your financial aid.
  5. This Grant is not available to residents of FL and is not applicable to students attending local campuses in OH.
  6. Except as expressly provided for herein, this Grant is valid for one Bachelor degree program only and cannot be combined with any other promotion or offer, with the exception of the Thinking Ahead Grant or the Student Body Tuition Freeze, unless otherwise specified in writing by the University.
  7. Employees of Apollo Group, Inc., its subsidiaries, their spouses and children are ineligible for this Grant.

University Representatives are not authorized to make modifications to this document, either orally or in writing, and any such attempted modification is not valid.

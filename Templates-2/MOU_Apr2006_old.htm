Axia College of University of Phoenix
Memorandum of Understanding April 2006 Promotion

Welcome to Axia College of University of Phoenix (WIU). We are excited to have you as part of our University family and wish you nothing but the best as you begin the process of accomplishing your educational goals.

Axia College takes pride in its ability to support both its existing student population and its prospective students with the highest level of customer service. In an effort to extend this level of service, we have offered to waive the application fee and Axcess fee for the first set of courses (IT/101 – Skills for Learning in an Information Age, and COM/110 – Effective, Persuasive Writing) at no charge to students beginning their academic programs during the month of April 2006.

Applications for admission must be received by noon MST on Monday, Apr 17, 2006. The final February start date is Monday, April 24, 2006.

The University wants to administer this benefit in a responsible and fair manner. For this reason, we are requesting that you enter into a formal agreement with us. By approving this document, you understand that if you are unable to start your first block of courses (IT/101 – Skills for Learning in an Information Age, and COM/110 – Effective, Persuasive Writing) during the month of April 2006, or are unable to successfully complete these courses, you will be billed the application fee of $45.00, and for the cost of your fee for the first set of courses.

Please note that this waiver is only applicable to first-time Axia College students who are beginning their first block of courses and a new degree program. This offer may not be applicable towards University of Phoenix campus-based programs.

We sincerely hope that this is the beginning of a relationship that provides you with the greatest possible success in both your personal and professional life.

*Promotion savings may not be applied to / does not include course tuition.
*Please note that this promotional offer cannot be extended to the employees of University of Phoenix / Apollo Group.
